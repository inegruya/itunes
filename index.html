    Dua lipa (Lipa - Albanees voor ‘ik hou van u’ ) is a british singer. Dua Lipa is well-known for her sultry vocals, the dark storylines and honest lyrics of her songs and her “progressive” pop sound. She describes her musical style as “dark pop”. Her biggest influence is Canadian pop singer Nelly Furtado. She also cites Pink, J. Cole, David Bowie, 2Pac, Kendrick Lamar, Bob Dylan, 50 Cent and A$AP Rocky as huge influences on her music, but also on the way she writes and sings.Na twee jaar is haar titelloze debuut eindelijk klaar.
